Welcome to Online Learning
Dean of Students · 4:32
Online courses aren't easier — they require more self-discipline and time management than many students expect. This module will set you up for success from day one.

How much time should you expect?

Per credit hour
Plan for 2–3 hours of total work per week for each credit hour you're enrolled in.
A 3-credit course
Expect roughly 6–9 hours per week — not counting the time watching lectures or videos.
Consistency wins
Shorter daily sessions outperform marathon cramming sessions every time.

What successful online students do consistently

  • Log in to Canvas every single day — even just to check announcements
  • Contact their instructor early when something is unclear, not the night before it's due
  • Treat the course like a scheduled commitment, not something to fit in "when there's time"
  • Ask for help before they fall behind — not after
  • Have a backup plan for technology problems (more on this in Module 2)
